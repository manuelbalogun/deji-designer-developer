A LITTLE ABOUT ME

Hello there!👋🏾‍️

Thanks for stopping by my little corner of the web. I hope you like it here :)

My name is Ayodeji Balogun (in no way related to Wizkid - bummer 😆), but please call me Deji. I am a product designer currently based in Nigeria. For some reason, as a kid I thought I was going to be an aeronautical engineer. Guess it sounded really cool at the time.

Now I draw rectangles and push pixels on Figma, Webflow and just about any technology out there that enables rectangle-drawing and pixel-pushing. In fancier terms, I enjoy creating delightful experiences for my fellow netizens, helping them solve a variety of problems with data-driven digital products, from simple websites to complex enterprise software.

Beyond work, I am a fitness aficionado, melophile, and aspiring polyglot (currently learning French, Spanish and German).

Got questions, or just wanna chat about design, fitness, or music? I'd love to hear from you!
